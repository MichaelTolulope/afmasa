AfMASA Events

Strategic Gatherings for Tangible Outcomes

Purpose-Driven Convenings

While AfMASA is a permanent advocacy platform, our events serve as the essential mechanisms for implementation, policy coordination, and high-level maritime dialogue across Africa.

The Annual Assembly (Flagship Event)

Continental Agenda Setting

Our premier platform for publishing public resolutions and shaping Africa’s maritime regulatory and investment priorities.

Ministerial & CEO-Level Dialogue

Ministers, regulators, CEOs, financiers, and global maritime institutions convene to translate policy into measurable economic outcomes.

Implementation Frameworks

Structured sessions designed to drive regulatory harmonization, infrastructure reform, and fleet competitiveness.

Past Highlight: AfMASA 2025 (London)

Hosted during London International Shipping Week, AfMASA 2025 established a continental consensus on regulatory harmonization and strengthened Africa–global maritime cooperation.

Upcoming: AfMASA 2026

The 2026 Assembly will focus on "De-risking Africa's Shipping Corridors", addressing insurance gaps, corridor security, port infrastructure, and capital mobilization strategies to unlock sustainable fleet expansion.

Regional Forums

Targeted engagements designed to drive localized implementation and stakeholder alignment.

East Africa Logistics Summits

Corridor optimization, port efficiency, customs harmonization, and trade facilitation across Eastern African markets.

West African Maritime Finance Roundtables
