About AFMASA

Building stronger maritime cooperation across Africa

The Africa Maritime and Shipping Assembly (AFMASA) is the platform to foster intra-African trade through enhanced shipping and maritime cooperation. We bring together industry leaders, government officials, and regulatory bodies to drive innovation, strengthen partnerships, and advance the maritime sector across the continent.
