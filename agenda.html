AFMASA 2025 Agenda

September 18, 2025 • International Maritime Organisation, London

Event Date & Location

Date: Thursday, September 18, 2025

Venue: International Maritime Organisation (IMO) Headquarters

Address: Albert Embankment, London, United Kingdom

Part of: London International Shipping Week (LISW) 2025

Full Event Schedule

A full day of engaging sessions, networking, and maritime industry insights

08:45 AM Arrival & Registration

Delegate Check-in

Welcome participants with coffee and registration. Network with fellow maritime professionals and industry leaders.

Main Foyer
09:30 AM - 11:00 AM 90 Minutes

Fueling Growth Through Commercial Shipping

A comprehensive discussion on practical solutions for enhancing intra-Africa trade and maritime cooperation.

Key Topics:

  • Optimizing shipping routes and schedules
  • Developing efficient port infrastructure and logistics networks
  • Leveraging technology to improve supply chain visibility and efficiency
  • Addressing financing and insurance challenges in African shipping

Participants:

  • Leading shipping companies
  • Logistics providers
  • Trade experts
  • Industry professionals
Main Conference Hall Panel Discussion
11:00 AM - 12:00 PM 60 Minutes

LRF Mini-Session

Exclusive session with LRF (Lloyd's Register Foundation) focusing on maritime excellence and professional development in the shipping industry.

Committee Rooms 11-13, IMO Limited Capacity
12:00 PM - 1:00 PM Break

Lunch & Networking

Enjoy a gourmet lunch while networking with fellow delegates, speakers, and maritime industry leaders. Excellent opportunity for one-on-one conversations.

Dining Area
1:00 PM - 3:00 PM 120 Minutes

Afternoon Sessions

Multiple parallel sessions covering specialized topics in maritime law, technology, sustainability, and regional development.

Various Conference Rooms
3:00 PM - 5:00 PM Evening Reception

Networking Reception & B2B Matchmaking

An elegant evening reception where delegates can meet with industry peers, potential partners, and service providers. Premium refreshments and networking activities.

Rooftop Terrace All Delegates Welcome
5:00 PM Closing Remarks

Event Closing & Announcements

Final remarks from AFMASA leadership, key announcements, and preview of next year's assembly. Thank you to all participants and sponsors.
